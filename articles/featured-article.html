NVIDIA & Intel: Stock Market Trends

February 2025 | Market Insights

Is NVIDIA Still King of AI Chips?

By Moon | Level Up News

NVIDIA has experienced an incredible surge in its stock value over the past year, largely driven by the explosive growth in AI technology and the company’s expansion into data centers. As AI applications become more integral to industries across the globe, NVIDIA’s cutting-edge GPUs have become essential in powering the next wave of machine learning, deep learning, and AI-driven innovations. Additionally, the company has made significant strides in its data center business, securing major deals with cloud providers and enterprises. This has positioned NVIDIA as a dominant player in both the gaming and enterprise sectors. Recently, NVIDIA announced the next-generation RTX 50 series, which promises to deliver even more powerful graphics processing capabilities. This, combined with their strategic AI partnerships with companies like Google and Microsoft, has only fueled further optimism among investors. As a result, many analysts remain highly bullish on NVIDIA’s future growth potential. However, with its stock already at a premium, some investors are wondering if there’s still room for growth or if the price might be due for a correction.

Intel's Comeback: Can It Challenge AMD & NVIDIA?

By Shihab | Level Up News

Intel has been making a lot of progress with its new Meteor Lake processors, hoping to regain some of the market share it’s lost in both the CPU and GPU sectors. The company has been putting a lot of money into improving chip production and boosting AI capabilities, which has got investors feeling more hopeful. Intel is focusing on making its processors more energy-efficient while also boosting their overall performance, aiming to challenge strong competitors like AMD and NVIDIA. Even though Intel had a strong financial quarter, some analysts still have doubts about whether it can catch up with NVIDIA, especially in the field of AI.
