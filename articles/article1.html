NVIDIA & Intel: Stock Market Trends

February 2025 | Market Insights

Is NVIDIA Still King of AI Chips?

By Riley | Level Up News

NVIDIA has seen an unprecedented rise in its stock value over the past year, fueled by the AI boom and data center expansion. With recent announcements of the next-gen RTX 50 series and strategic AI partnerships, investors remain bullish on its growth.

However, concerns over semiconductor shortages and increasing competition from AMD and Intel may pose challenges. Will NVIDIA sustain its dominance?

Intel's Comeback: Can It Challenge AMD & NVIDIA?

By Riley | Level Up News

Intel has been making strides with its new Meteor Lake processors, aiming to reclaim lost market share in the CPU and GPU sectors. The company’s investments in chip manufacturing and AI acceleration have sparked investor optimism.

Despite a strong quarter, some analysts remain skeptical about Intel’s ability to match NVIDIA’s lead in AI processing. Will 2025 be Intel’s comeback year?
